About Me

Picture Credit: Ilani Casino Resorts, Washington.

The name is Navneet Prakash and I’m 26 years old. I was born on the beautiful island of Fiji. Moving from Fiji over to America was a life changing experience for me. But i take it as an opportunity that has opened the doors of education and build more upon my carrer.

Now, I’m consistently learning web technologies and other design related topics, currently expanding my knowledge in Business Web Practices, Python Program, Database and WordPress .

Nowadays I spend my day mostly in College College either coding or in class learning something new. Im heading for my AAT in Web Development.
