our Mission

PureKraze organizes and hosts events for Veterans that honor them and also help them with getting resources for their well-being such as food and medical help. PureKraze events for Veterans also brings to public awareness the needs and issues faced by Veterans.

Many Veterans need our help today and most of what’s needed is to rebuild their lives, starting with recovery and wellness.

Veterans Events

PureKraze holds commemorative events for Veterans and their families several times a year.
Veterans and/or their families are honored at these ceremonies.

Learn More

We will never be able to repay our Veterans or give them back what they have lost.

However we can show our support and let them know that we honor them through our program, 
and can help connect them with resources.
